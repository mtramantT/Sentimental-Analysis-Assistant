About Meraki

Social media has become very popular. Having a social media has its advantages such as allowing a individual to stay connected to their family and friends, gives us enjoyment, its a fast instant communication, and it is great for promoting a business. Just as well as social media has its advantages, it also has its disadvantages. Social Media disadvantages can cause a individual to have social peer pressure, online interaction substitution for offline interaction, also distraction and procrasination. With this application you will be able to create a blog about how you feel after you visit social media. By blogging about your feelings after you visit social media will create a sentimental analysis, which you will be able to better control your mood, and to see how social media is affecting your life. By viewing your sentimental analysis, you will have a better idea on how social media is affecting your life, and you can slowly decrease the amount of time you are on it.
